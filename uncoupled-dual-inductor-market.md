<p><ul><li>Radial</li><li>SMD/SMT</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Uncoupled dual inductors are categorized into three main market types: radial, SMD/SMT, and through-hole. Radial inductors have leads that extend from the body, allowing for easy insertion into PCBs, making them suitable for space-constrained applications. SMD/SMT inductors are designed for surface mounting, enabling higher density and automation in assembly. Through-hole inductors, with their long leads, provide robust connections and are commonly used in heavy-duty applications. Each type serves specific electronic design needs in various industries.</p></p>
