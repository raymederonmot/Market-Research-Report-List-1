<p><p>T-slot cylinder sensors are essential components used in automation for detecting the position of actuators. The market is primarily divided into PNP and NPN cylinder sensors. PNP sensors are designed to deliver a positive voltage signal when activated, making them compatible with positive supply voltage systems. Conversely, NPN sensors provide a negative signal output, suitable for systems where grounding is required. Both types cater to different industrial applications, offering solutions for efficient monitoring and control in various automation setups.</p></p>
