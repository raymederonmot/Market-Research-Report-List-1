<p><ul><li>A Type</li><li>M Type</li><li>B Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Echo type diagnostic ultrasound devices are categorized into three main types: A-type, M-type, and B-type. A-type ultrasound produces a single-dimensional graphical representation of echoes, primarily used for measuring distances and assessing tissue characteristics. M-type ultrasound offers a time-motion display, useful in cardiac assessments to visualize motion over time. B-type, also known as 2D ultrasound, creates cross-sectional images of the body, providing detailed structural information. Each type serves distinct diagnostic purposes across various medical fields.</p></p>
