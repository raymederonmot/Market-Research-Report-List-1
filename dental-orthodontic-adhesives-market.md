<p><p>The dental orthodontic adhesives market comprises several types based on curing mechanisms. Light-cured adhesives utilize light activation to cure quickly, offering convenience and efficiency during procedures. Dual-cured adhesives combine both methods, allowing flexibility in application, as they can cure both with light and chemically.
