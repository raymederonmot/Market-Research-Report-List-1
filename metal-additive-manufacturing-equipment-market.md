<p><p>The Metal Additive Manufacturing Equipment market is primarily categorized into Selective Laser Melting (SLM), Electron Beam Melting (EBM), and other methods. SLM utilizes high-powered lasers to fuse metal powders layer by layer, achieving complex geometries with high precision. EBM employs electron beams in a vacuum, offering greater energy efficiency and superior thermal control, ideal for high-performance materials. Other methods include binder jetting and directed energy deposition, each catering to specific applications and material requirements in various industries.</p></p>
