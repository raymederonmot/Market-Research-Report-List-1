<p><p>The 3D printed digital dentures market comprises two primary types: full dentures and partial dentures. Full dentures are designed to replace all missing teeth in an arch, providing a complete solution for those who have lost their natural teeth. In contrast, partial dentures are used when some natural teeth remain, filling gaps and restoring functionality while maintaining the integrity of existing teeth. Both types benefit from advanced 3D printing technology, allowing for precise customization, improved fit, and quicker production times.</p></p>
