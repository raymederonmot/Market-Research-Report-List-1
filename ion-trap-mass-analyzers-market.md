<p><p>Ion trap mass analyzers are categorized into several types, primarily including quadrupole ion trap and linear ion trap mass analyzers. Quadrupole ion trap analyzers utilize electric fields to confine ions, enabling effective mass filtering and analysis, making them popular for routine applications. Linear ion traps, on the other hand, allow for prolonged ion storage and enhanced mass resolution, ideal for complex mixtures. Other emerging technologies also contribute to the market, offering innovative solutions for specific analytical needs in various industries.</p></p>
