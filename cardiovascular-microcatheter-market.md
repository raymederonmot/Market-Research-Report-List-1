<p><p>The cardiovascular microcatheter market consists of two main types: single lumen and dual lumen microcatheters. Single lumen microcatheters are designed with one channel, allowing for a straightforward delivery of therapeutic agents or diagnostic tools. In contrast, dual lumen microcatheters feature two separate channels, enabling simultaneous functions such as infusion and aspiration. This versatility enhances procedural efficiency, making dual lumen options ideal for complex interventions. Both types are crucial in navigating small and intricate vascular structures during cardiovascular procedures.</p></p>
